## Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Blue**: #2563eb (Primary buttons, links)
- **Purple**: #7c3aed (Accents, gradients)
- **Slate**: #1e293b (Dark backgrounds)

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: #10b981 (Verified, approved)
- **Warning**: #f59e0b (Reports, alerts)
- **Error**: #ef4444 (Delete, ban, errors)
- **Info**: #3b82f6 (Informational)

### Neutral Colors
- **White**: #ffffff (Backgrounds)
- **Gray-50**: #f9fafb (Light backgrounds)
- **Gray-600**: #4b5563 (Secondary text)
- **Gray-900**: #111827 (Primary text)

### Dark Mode
- **Dark-800**: #1f2937
- **Dark-900**: #111827
- **Dark-950**: #030712

---

## Interactive States

### Buttons
- **Default**: Gradient from blue to purple
- **Hover**: Darker gradient, slight scale
- **Active**: Pressed state
- **Disabled**: Grayed out, no interaction

### Cards
- **Default**: White background, subtle shadow
- **Hover**: Increased shadow, slight lift
- **Dark Mode**: Dark-900 background

### Icons
- **Default**: Gray-600
- **Active**: Blue-600
- **Hover**: Darker shade

---

## Typography

### Font Sizes
- **Heading 1**: 2xl-4xl (responsive)
- **Heading 2**: xl-2xl
- **Body**: base (16px)
- **Small**: sm (14px)
- **Extra Small**: xs (12px)

### Font Weights
- **Bold**: 700 (headings, emphasis)
- **Semibold**: 600 (subheadings)
- **Medium**: 500 (labels)
- **Regular**: 400 (body text)

---

## Animation & Transitions

### Motion
- **Page Enter**: Fade in + slide up (y: 20)
- **Stagger**: Delay index * 0.05s
- **Duration**: 0.2-0.3s
- **Easing**: Spring physics

### Hover Effects
- **Scale**: 1.05 (buttons)
- **Shadow**: Increase elevation
- **Color**: Darken/lighten


### Loading States
- **Spinner**: Rotating circle
- **Skeleton**: Pulsing gray boxes
- **Progress**: Linear bar
